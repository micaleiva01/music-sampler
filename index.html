C O L D P L A Y

Coldplay is a british band composed by vocalist and pianist Chris Martin, bassist Guy Berryman, drummer Will Champion and guitarist Jonny Buckland. According to this Wikipedia page, the band was born in 1996 when they all met in college in London.

Coldplay rose to fame in the year 2000 when they released their debut album Parachutes that includes their hit single Yellow. The album received a Brit Award for British Album of the Year and a Grammy Award for Best Alternative Music Album. Their other albums, such as A Rush of Blood to the Head, X & Y, Mylo Xyloto, and Ghost Stories have also been incredibly successful all over the world and have won many awards as well.

The band has had a huge influence in modern music. As stated by journalist Felipe Branco Cruz, "Coldplay reinvented the concept of arena rock", according to the Wikipedia page. Coldplay started using LED wristbands in their concerts which is a trend that many artists, such as Taylor Swift and Lady Gaga, have now adopted for their concerts as well.

To learn more about them visit their official website here: Coldplay.com
